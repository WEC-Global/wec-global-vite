Our Story

Early 2021, a few wonderful women, from different parts of the world, came together to build something special. We, as women in the fields of ethics and compliance, wanted to create a trusted circle of inspiring women celebrating the essence of all that is best for professional women.

Each of the founding members of WEC Global has achieved something unique in her own life and, by coming together, we knew we could achieve something even greater. Using digital technology in creative ways, we aspire to create an invaluable network of peers, learning, and career-building without borders and biases.

Through professional, social, and personal growth opportunities, we are here to support you at every step of your journey. Our aim is to encourage a mutually collaborative spirit to nurture and support the success and fulfillment of each individual by providing a unique platform to connect and collaborate freely. We are able to do this through a private online community, personal one-to-one introductions, mentorship programs, writing opportunities, and exclusive events hosted by WEC Global and our members.

We pride ourselves on being an inclusive, rather than exclusive, organization. Whatever part of the world you come from or skillset within ethics and compliance you hold, you are welcome to apply. Men are also absolutely welcome to join as a friend of WEC Global. We welcome you to stand firm with us as we globally unite in compliance and ethics.

Mission

To build a network of compliance and ethics professional women that is structured around mutual professional trust and provide a safe space for women to promote their compliance careers, share experiences, build friendships, and establish partnerships.

Vision

To inspire women in fostering change towards inclusivity, advancing personal development, and breaking barriers within ethics and compliance globally.
