Changing the landscape

To reach a conscious inclusion of diversity and gender, we all must act as a catalyst in encouraging women to reach new heights. That is why at WEC Global, we work with people based on their commitment to our mission, regardless of their gender and background. Men are also absolutely welcome to join as a friend of WEC Global.

Here are a few reasons why you should join us:

Speak at events and participate in certain Members and Friends events.

Write blogs for our website and contribute to the network.

Share job openings and create opportunities for women of WEC Global.

Eligibility criteria to join as a friend

As Friends of WEC Global, you can contribute to our network and gain limited access to events and initiatives open for discussion and idea-sharing, even if you cannot qualify for membership. There is no geographic specification to this initiative. You need not be based in a specific region for your application to be accepted.

Registration

It is free to join our network, so why not join today?

Before applying, please ensure that you have read, understood, and agreed upon our Code of Ethics and Privacy Statement, which can be found on our website.
